# LOAD-BEARING
*A column about what homes hold, how spaces are cleared, and why it matters.*

---

**By Alicia Malay**

I've spent over a decade in luxury residential real estate — telling the
stories of homes and the people who inhabit them. What I kept watching,
in every listing, every transition, every estate sale, was the one element
no one was addressing. The energy a space holds long after the people
inside it have gone.

MOKSHA is the practice I built around that. Load-Bearing is where I write
about it. Property by property. What I find in a space, what surprises me,
and what I'm learning about why some homes move and others don't.
